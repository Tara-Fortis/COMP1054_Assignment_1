About Gramercy Park

When I first heard this song, I thought it was about a woman changing who she is to be what her partner wants her to be. But now I think it is more about how fame forces artists to change who they are to fit into what their fans want them to be, that the person that the audience sees is a reflection of what they want to see.

Alicia Keys

Alicia Keys is an American singer and songwriter. She was born in Hell’s Kitchen in Manhattan, New York City, on January 25, 1981. From an early age, Keys loved music; she discovered her passion for the piano at age 6. At 10, she received a piano from a neighbor, on which she would practice daily.
